# Kairos Analytics

**On-chain behavioral analytics for Web3 applications.**

## Why Kairos Analytics?

Traditional analytics tools store your data on their servers. They can be blocked by ad blockers, disabled by privacy regulations, or simply taken down. Kairos Analytics routes events through a lightweight relayer that batches and commits them to the blockchain â€” your data belongs to you, forever.

| | Traditional Analytics | Kairos Analytics |
|---|---|---|
| Data storage | Third-party servers | Base mainnet (you own it) |
| Censorable | Yes | No |
| Blockable by ad blockers | Yes | No |
| Privacy (PII) | Complex compliance | No PII collected |
